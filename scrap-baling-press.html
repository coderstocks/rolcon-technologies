Double Compression

A Double Compression Scrap Baling Press Machine is a robust industrial machine designed for compacting and bundling scrap materials, such as metals, plastics, and other recyclables, into dense bales. These machines use high pressure to reduce the volume of scrap material, making it easier to handle, transport, and recycle.

Car Baler

Car Baler Scrap Baling Press Machine is a specialized industrial machine designed for compressing and baling end-of-life vehicles (ELVs) and other large, bulky scrap materials into compact bales. These machines are essential in the recycling industry for efficiently processing automotive scrap, reducing its volume for easier handling, transportation, and further recycling processes.

Continuous Baler

They are used to process the metal scrap in fully automated mode with continuous feeding, baling, and ejection. These are best suited for homogenous metal scraps like UBCs (Used beverage cans), CRC and SS punching & Sheets, and other such materials. To give our customers bales with the highest density and high productivity.

Fodder Baler

A Fodder Baler Scrap Baling Press Machine, also known as a Hay Baler or Silage Baler, is a specialized machine used to compress and package fodder, hay, straw, and other agricultural residues into compact, easily transportable bales. These machines are vital for efficiently managing and storing animal feed and agricultural by-products.

Auto Tie Paper Baler

An Auto Tie Paper Baler is a specialized machine designed for the efficient handling, compacting, and binding of paper waste into dense, transportable bales. These balers are widely used in recycling facilities, printing industries, and waste management centers to streamline the process of managing large volumes of paper and cardboard waste.
