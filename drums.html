Industrial Cable Drums

Industrial cable drums, also known as cable reels or spools, are large cylindrical or disc-shaped objects used to store, transport, and dispense cables and wires in various industrial applications. These drums are designed to accommodate large lengths and diameters of cables, providing a convenient and efficient solution for managing cable assemblies in industrial settings.

Process Cable Drum

Process cable drums are commonly used in manufacturing facilities and assembly lines to supply materials such as electrical cables, pneumatic hoses, hydraulic lines, and conveyor belts to production machinery. They facilitate continuous production by providing a steady and controlled feed of materials to various stages of the manufacturing process.

Automatic Drum Crusher Set

An automatic drum crusher set is a specialized equipment used in industrial settings for compacting and disposing of large quantities of drums or barrels. These sets typically consist of a drum crusher machine, associated conveyor systems, and other accessories designed to automate the process of crushing and compacting drums for disposal or recycling.

Heavy Duty Steel Cable Drum

A heavy-duty steel cable drum, also known as a cable reel or spool, is a robust and durable cylindrical object typically made of steel and used to store, transport, and dispense large lengths of cables or wires. These cable drums are designed to withstand heavy loads, provide stability during transportation, and ensure the safe handling of cables in various industrial applications.

Read More

Mild Steel Cable Drum

A mild steel cable drum, also known simply as a cable drum or spool, is a cylindrical object made of mild steel and used for storing, transporting, and dispensing cables or wires in various industrial applications. While not as robust as heavy-duty steel drums, mild steel cable drums offer a cost-effective solution for handling cables in applications where heavy loads or extreme conditions are not a concern.
